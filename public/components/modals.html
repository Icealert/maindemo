Device Setup Guide

Step-by-step instructions for installing your FreezeSense device

1 Unboxing Contents

  • FreezeSense monitoring unit
  • Temperature sensor array
  • Power adapter with 6ft cable
  • Mounting brackets & hardware

2 Sensor Placement

Optimal sensor positions for accurate readings:

Do:

  • Mount 2-3 inches above ice level
  • Keep sensors clean and dry

Don't:

  • Place near heat sources
  • Submerge in water

3 Power & Connectivity

Connection Steps:

  1. Plug in power adapter
  2. Wait for blue LED indicator
  3. Connect to FreezeSense WiFi network
  4. Follow in-app setup instructions
