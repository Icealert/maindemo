Frequently Asked Questions

How difficult is it to install FreezeSense sensors?

FreezeSense sensors are designed for easy installation. Most customers can install the system themselves in under an hour with our step-by-step guide

What type of alerts will I receive?

You'll receive real-time alerts for temperature variations, water flow issues, power interruptions, and dropped connection. Alerts can be customized and delivered via email, or SMS.

How many ice machines can I monitor with one account?

Our platform scales to your needs. You can monitor a single machine or hundreds across multiple locations. All your data is accessible from a single dashboard with location-based organization.

How can FreezeSense add value to my business?

Each prevented failure saves over $500, about $200 in emergency ice and $300 in dispatch and labor.

What does implementing FreezeSense cost?

There is a one-time hardware cost, and a low monthly subscription per device for dashboard access & cloud storage. If you'd like to implement FreezeSense at scale, please reach out to discuss bulk discounts!
